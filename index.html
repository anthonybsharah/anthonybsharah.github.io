About Me

I’m Zeid, an upcoming 2026 Political Science graduate from the University of California, Los Angeles.

I studied political science at UCLA, where my coursework spanned international law, political theory, and the relationship between legal institutions and public policy. Alongside my studies, I interned at a Sacramento law firm, where I produced legal memoranda and analyses for an active litigation team, and served as an elected Student Senator at my community college.

I am drawn to the intersection of law, policy, and advocacy, particularly the ways legal systems shape the lives of everyday people. I am currently working towards a JD, but want to take time to build my career, connections, and experiences before entering law school.

Outside of my academics and work life, I enjoy soccer, exploring music from different cultures, learning new languages, traveling, and photography to document my travels and experiences.
